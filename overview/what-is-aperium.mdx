Aperium is an enterprise software platform that uses artificial intelligence to help organizations manage and automate core business operations. The platform integrates with existing business systems and workflows to support operational efficiency across the enterprise.

## Core capabilities

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Agentic OS: Orchestration Layer">
    An intelligent orchestration layer with built-in understanding of your company's tools and workflows. Any user can interact with underlying systems, compose workflows, and create agents, without needing to understand those systems directly.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Ontology Engine: Knowledge Graph Layer">
    We ingest structured and unstructured data across your systems and build a semantic understanding of how your company actually operates, not just the data, but the meaning behind it. How your team uses tables. How finance builds forecasts. How product decisions get made. Encoded into a live knowledge graph.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Predictive Intelligence: Analysis Layer">
    On top of this foundation, we proactively surface risks, opportunities, and recommended actions. Anomaly detection and learned patterns from your company's own data, automatically generating insights before you know to ask for them.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Why Aperium is different

Most enterprise AI is a thin wrapper on a general purpose model. It doesn't know what your company actually does. Aperium is different: we build a living semantic model of how an organization operates, its terminology, data relationships, and decision logic, and put that understanding at the center of every workflow, prediction, and agent.
